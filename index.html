vodQA Bangalore

vodQA is a forum for passionate quality enthusiasts to network, share and learn from each other. So far, we have successfully hosted more than 20+ editions of vodQA across all ThoughtWorks India offices. vodQA aims at taking you to another level altogether, where you get a wider perspective of testing. Let us meet more evangelists, to talk and learn about the vast spectrum of testing realms.

We cordially invite the attendees to register with us. Please have a look at the Agenda below.
