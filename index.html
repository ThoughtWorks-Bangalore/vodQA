GET HIGH ON TESTING

vodQA - Get high on testing, aims at taking you to another level altogether, where you get a wider perspective of testing. Let us meet more evangelists, to talk and learn about the vast spectrum of testing realms.

We cordially invite the speaker in you to share and validate your knowledge with the enthusiastic QA community. You could submit talks related to, but not limited to, Consulting, Data Analytics testing, Security testing, Web service testing etc.

We have 3 formats:
Presentation - duration: 30 mins (+ 15 mins Q&A)
Lightning talks - duration: 15 mins
Workshops - duration: 90 mins
We encourage you to make as many submissions as possible.

Go ahead and register yourself with us.
The last date for submissions is 13th June 2016.

We will be opening up attendee registrations in a short while.
