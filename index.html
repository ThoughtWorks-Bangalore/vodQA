Trends in Testing

This edition of vodQA aims at discussing various trends in software testing driven by newer technologies in this era of business digitalization and mobilization. We cordially invite the speaker in you to share and validate your knowledge with the enthusiastic QA community. You could submit talks related to any vertical of testing on Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Security, Web services, Mobile, IoT, Robotic Process Automation(RPA) and more.


vodQA is a forum for testing enthusiasts, working on latest developments from the world of software testing. Here, one meets with old and new counterparts, exchanges ideas and learnings with the larger testing community.
