GET HIGH ON TESTING

ThoughtWorks is pleased to have successfully hosted another edition of vodQA in Bangalore. This edition of vodQA, aimed at taking you to another level altogether, where you get a wider perspective of testing.


vodQA is a forum for testing enthusiasts, working on latest developments from the world of software testing. Here, one meets with old and new counterparts, exchanges ideas and learnings with the larger testing community.
